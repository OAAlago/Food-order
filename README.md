
## Food order analysis cost and profitability

### Data Source and Overview

The dataset used for this analysis was gotten from the Kaggle.com website. It contains 1001 rows and 12 columns and it covers the period from January to February 2024

### Objectives

- Analyze delivery performance and efficiency across payment methods and order values
- Assess revenue, costs, and profitability by payment method
- Evaluate the impact of discount types on order value and refund rates
- Examine processing fee costs across different payment channels

### Tools

  6. Delivery time by highest refunds
      - insights; 76 minutes is the time with the highest refunds


     ### Recommendations
  - Encourage Digital Payment Methods
     -  Offer a small loyalty bonus (e.g., 20 cashback) for Digital Wallet payments in order to add volume to Cash on Delivery. This will boost the Digital payments method.
  - Investigate High Refund Orders
     -  A further analysis can be performed to investigate the reason for the high refund orders to find the root cause. By reducing the refunds, this would increase the revenue of the restaurants
  - Discontinue ineffective discount
     - Based on the analysis, the 15% new user discount should be discontinued as it has a high refund rate 
  - High-value low-speed orders
     - 	Certain high value orders have low delivery speed. The restaurants should consider other options to ensure fast delivery speed
